Return/Replacement Policy for Buyers

This Returns/Replacement Policy (“Policy”) will govern request to return/replacement any of the product (“Product”) purchased by buyer (referred to as “Buyer”, you, your, etc.) on www.elektrifix.com (“Us”). For the purposes of this Policy, the term Buyer will be referred to as “User”.

1. Our return/replacement policy shall be applicable only for the following reasons. Return request shall be only be entertained if the product is delivered with physical damage. For all the other reasons as mentioned below only replacement shall be done depending on the reasons.

(a) Warranty issues with respect to the Product
(b) Wrong Product or Product not matching the description or specifications mentioned on the listing page on the Platform.
(c) Missing Product or some items from the entire order placed found to be missing due to reasons attributable to logistics provider.
(d) Damage to the outer box delivered or product damaged in transit or any other logistics related issues.

2. Depending on the reason, you can raise a return request for any of the issues as set forth under point 1. above within the timelines prescribed below.

(a) Product with physical damage: 1 day from the date of delivery of shipment
(b) Warranty: As per the warranty guidelines provided by the product manufacturer.
(c) Wrong Product or Product not matching the description or specs: 1 day from the date of delivery of shipment
(d) Missing Products or some items missing: 2 days from the date of delivery of shipment and only missing items shall be shipped if approved
(e) Damage to outer box or Product damage in transit.

3. Your return/replacement request will either be approved or rejected and the same will be communicated to you.

4. If the return/replacement request of the Buyer has been rejected due to the following reasons that:

(a) Buyer does not respond to the inquiry calls and/or calls made by us to procure missing documentation and information; or
(b) supportive documents are insufficient, or Buyer is unable to provide sufficient proof in support of the claim, Buyer may raise a new return request within three (2) days from the date of such rejection.

5. If your return/replacement request is approved except for the Warranty related replacemen. In all approved returns, refund will be initiated to you.

6. In such exceptional cases covered in para 5 above, payment will be initiated to you within forty-eight (48) banking hours

(a) from the date of approval of the return request by us; or
(b) from the date you confirm the dispatch of the Product from your shop/ designated pick-up location; whichever is later.

7. At the time of raising a dispute, Seller will be required to provide documents/ proof in support of the claim, which include without limitation, images of the returned product(s) indicating the issue in the shipment received. The images need to clearly capture the following:

(a) the Return ID
(b) AWB number of the shipment
(c) damages to the returned product
(d) brand name/ manufacturer’s name of the returned product

8. Any capitalised terms used herein this Policy and not defined explicitly shall have the same meaning as defined in the Terms of Use.

9. User agrees to be bound to any changes or modifications made to this Policy as may be updated on the Platform, from time to time or any additional terms that may be communicated to you, from time to time.
