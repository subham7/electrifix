Elektrifix is a B2B
e-commerce platform

designed to provide retailers and businesses across India access to Electrical Goods of various brands under a single roof

It provides the Manufacturers and importers of Electrical goods a ready market to showcase their products and find buyers for their brand. It is the largest such platform for Electrical Goods for both industrial and domestic segments. It uses technology to create a niche and clutter free market for Electrical Goods.

Designed to be amazingly
simple yet robust

We know exactly how Electrical Goods are traded and what are the sellers and buyers looking for, thus making it extremely easy for them to:
i) BUY & SELL
ii) REACH new markets
iii) GROW their business

We negotiate the best rates for retailers and businesses, ensure that they get the right product in the quickest possible time. We take care of the logistics and provide a secure payment interface and as you earn vintage on our platform we provide you financing facility through our elektriFinance program. We provide the manufacturer and importers of Electrical Goods to showcase their products to a pan India audience through our simple listing process and various services like elektriLaunches, elektriPromos etc.
